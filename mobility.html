Pillar · Yoga & Mobility

Move well, recover faster.

Strength and miles build the engine — mobility, yoga, and breath keep it running clean. Every Elite Metabolix build programs movement work to protect your joints, speed recovery, and keep you on the road. Here's the toolkit.

01 — The Flows

Four sequences, mapped to your week.

Short, repeatable, and built around training — not a separate hobby to find time for. Each flow is cued inside your plan — tap any move below to see a demo.

Pre-Session Activation

8 MIN
Before runs & lifts
  • World's-greatest-stretch ×5/side
  • Leg swings — front & lateral ×10
  • Hip-opener lunge with reach
  • Ankle rocks + calf prep
  • 2 min easy nasal-breathing build

Post-Run Reset

10 MIN
After hard efforts
  • Downward dog → calf pedals
  • Low lunge + quad reach / side
  • Pigeon for hips ×90s/side
  • Figure-4 glute release
  • Legs-up-the-wall, slow exhale

Rest-Day Yoga

20 MIN
Off / recovery days
  • Gentle vinyasa to warm tissue
  • Hip + hamstring deep holds
  • Thoracic rotations for posture
  • Balance work (single-leg holds)
  • 5 min guided relaxation

Desk & Commute Mobility

5 MIN
Long sitting / drives
  • Standing hip-flexor opener
  • Seated spinal twists
  • Calf raises + ankle pumps
  • Chest & shoulder doorway stretch
  • Reset breath, 6 slow cycles
02 — Breathwork

The fastest lever you own.

Breath is the one recovery tool that works in seconds — downshifting your nervous system before sleep, between intervals, or in a stressful moment. Three patterns we program:

  • 1
    Box breath (4·4·4·4) — focus & pre-session calm. Inhale 4, hold 4, exhale 4, hold 4.
  • 2
    Physiological sigh — double inhale, long exhale. The quickest way to drop tension on demand.
  • 3
    4·7·8 wind-down — inhale 4, hold 7, exhale 8. Pairs with the sleep protocol in your plan.
