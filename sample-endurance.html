03 — A Real Fortnight

Built around a 7-on / 7-off shift.

Quality work is reserved for the off week, when sleep is normal. Work weeks hold the engine without digging a hole — runs land at 11:30 AM, after waking and before the evening shift.

05 — Recovery & Durability

The work only counts if you adapt to it.

Sleep on a night schedule

PROTECT THE 3A–10A WINDOW
  • Amber blue-light glasses from shift-end → sleep
  • Blackout curtains, cool room 65–68°F
  • Strategic pre-shift nap 20–30 min
  • Off-week: shift sleep earlier with AM bright light
  • Sleep aids / melatonin timing confirm w/ your physician

Injury-proofing a 6'2" frame

STAY ON THE ROAD
  • Never raise weekly volume > 10%
  • Eccentric calf (Alfredson) 3×15, 2×/day
  • Target cadence 170–180 spm
  • Rotate 2+ cushioned pairs; trails over concrete
  • Long-commute weeks: movement break every 90 min
